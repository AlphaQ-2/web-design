About Us

One of the three cities in the province of Batangas, Lipa City, a.k.a the “Little Rome of the Philippines,“ is a place with:

  • Magnificent churches, convents, retreat facilities, and seminaries;
  • Two popular apparitions (Shower of Petals in 1948 and the “Lady” atop a coconut tree in 1993); and
  • Great opportunities for investments and light industries.

Given its location, it is home to several tourist destinations such as Lipa City Community Park, Bluroze Farm, Mount Malarayat Golf and Country Club, The Farm at San Benito, Metropolitan Cathedral of San Sebastian, Carmel of Our Lady of All Grace, Divino Amor Chapel (Redemptorist Church), Mary Mediatrix of All Grace Parish, St. Therese of the Child Jesus Parish, Capuchin Chapel (St. Padre Pio), and Benedictine of the Incarcate World (OSB).
